Catch certificates

You must add catch certificates for all fish species unless they are exempt from illegal, unreported and unregulated (IUU) fishing controls.

For help with catch certificates, check the guidance on importing or moving fish into the UK (opens in new tab).

If you do not have catch certificates now

If catch certificates are missing or incomplete, the consignment is at high risk of being rejected.

Make sure you provide all required documents for this notification promptly to avoid disruptions and delays in the clearance process.
