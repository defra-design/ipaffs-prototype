Provide your supporting documents

! Warning Uploading documentary evidence is a legal requirement.

You must upload:

  • a health certificate (where required) – this must be in English
  • a journey log (where required)
  • other documents required for this consignment – this may include import licences or authorisations, declarations, commercial documents or invoices

Help with additional documents you must upload
In some cases, you’ll also need to upload documents such as:
  • an exporter’s declaration that animals are fit to travel, if no health certificate is required
  • a bluetongue declaration GBHC172 for livestock transiting bluetongue restricted territories
  • a RM39 licence and supplementary health certificate for rodents imported for research purposes
For more details about required documents, check the import rules you must follow in the IPAFFS guidance on GOV.UK (opens in new tab).
