Transport

Transporter

Your transporter must hold a valid transporter authorisation, issues by DAERA or APHA in the UK if they are:

  • transporting any live vertebrate animals in, to, from or through Great Britain (GB)
  • travelling on journeys of over 65 km
  • transporting as part of a economic (commercial) activity

Find out how to transport animals in connection with a economic activity (opens in a new tab)

Documents issued by DAERA are valid for use in GB.
Documents issued in any EU Member State are not valid for use in GB.
