Catch certificates

You must add catch certificates for fish processed in a different country to the catching vessel’s flag state, unless exempt.

This is to comply with Illegal, unreported and unregulated (IUU) fishing controls.

For help with required documents, check the guidance on importing or moving fish into the UK (opens in new tab).
