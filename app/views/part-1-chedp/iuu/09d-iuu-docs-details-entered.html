Illegal, unreported and unregulated (IUU) fishing documents

Make sure you upload all required IUU documents or the consignment may be rejected.

For help with required documents, check the guidance on importing or moving fish into the UK (opens in new tab).

The documents must be:

  • smaller than 10MB
  • a DOC, PDF, XLS, JPEG, PNG or GIF file

Inform us if your consignment has more than a 100 documents.
