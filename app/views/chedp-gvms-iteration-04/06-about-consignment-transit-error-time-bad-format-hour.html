About the consignment

What is the main reason for importing the consignment?

For imports of products intended for sale or use in Great Britain (England, Scotland or Wales).

Purpose in the internal market

You’ll find this on the transit declaration. This is 18 characters and made up of numbers and letters, for example, 23GB1234567890ABCD.
For consignments intended for direct travel to a third county, that will stay only within the same port or airport in Great Britain while moving to another means of transport.
For consignments moving through Great Britain for direct travel to a third country, that will enter Great Britain at one port or airport and leave from a different one within England, Scotland or Wales.

This applies only to goods moved under the Common Transit Convention (CTC) and will be on the transit declaration. This is 18 characters and made up of numbers and letters, for example, 23GB1234567890ABCD.
When the consignment will leave Great Britain
For example, 27 3 2023. This must be within 7 days of arrival in Great Britain.
24 hour format, for example, 14 50

Error: Time must include hour

Transited country
For consignments authorised for re-entry, or rejected exports re-entering Great Britain.
