About the consignment

Purpose and regulatory requirements

What is the main reason for importing the consignment?
The consignment is intended for sale or use in Great Britain (England, Scotland or Wales).

Purpose in the internal market

The consignment will stay within the same port or airport in Great Britain. It will change means of transport, then travel to a third country.
The consignment will move through Great Britain. It will enter and leave through different ports or airports, then travel to a third country.
When the consignment will leave Great Britain For example, 27 3 2023. This must be within 7 days of arrival in Great Britain.

Does the consignment comply with the regulatory requirements?

If the goods are for the internal market, transit, transhipment or movement for ship supply, they must comply with regulatory requirements specified in points (a) and (d) of Assimilated Regulation 2017/625.

Transits, transhipments, or movement for ship supply may not need to meet the regulatory requirements, if not intended for the internal market.
