Retrospective cloning - CHEDA only

This is for live animal importers who only have access to the health certificate as the animals are boarding the vehicle from the exporting country.

The notifier must submit a CHED within the timeframe the ports need it by. Then, once the notifier has the digital health certificate, they can clone details from it into the previously submitted notification.
