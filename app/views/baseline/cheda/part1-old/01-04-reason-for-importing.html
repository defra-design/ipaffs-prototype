About the consignment

What is the main reason for importing the animals?

For imports of animals intended for sale or use in Great Britain (England, Scotland or Wales).
Purpose in the internal market
Animals that will be sold in Great Britain, are already sold and being moved to a new owner, or being moved to a new owner with no sale involved.
For exhibition, use in zoo, collection, or conservation where a licence or approval is needed.
A pet of any species moving with their owner, or being moved by a commercial transporter to relocate with their owner.
Live animals that are farmed for food or fibre, such as for the production of milk, eggs or wool.
For animals intended for direct travel to a third country, that will stay only within the same port or airport in Great Britain while moving to another means of transport.
For animals moving through Great Britain for direct travel to a third country, that will enter Great Britain at one port or airport and leave from a different one within England, Scotland or Wales.
