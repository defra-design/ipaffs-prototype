Transport to the port of entry

Means of transport to port of entry

To identify the means of transport, enter (one of the following):

  • flight number
  • train number
  • road vehicle registration number
  • vessel name (for ferries, also the road vehicle registration number)

Are any road trailers or shipping containers being used to transport the consignment?

Provide details of all road trailers and containers or your consignment may be delayed. You may need to contact your haulier for this.
Enter the container identification number, or the trailer registration number or number plate.
Enter the seal number on the official certificate or any other seal mentioned in the accompanying documents.
An official seal is affixed under the supervision of the competent authority issuing the certificate.
