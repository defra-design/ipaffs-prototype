Clone from your ePhyto

You can copy the data and documents from your ePhyto into your import notification.

What is cloning?

Cloning can save you time by reducing manual errors and border checks that are triggered when data doesn't match up. It involves the following steps:

  1. You enter the ePhyto reference number
  2. It sets up a link between the exporter's system (such as Traces NT) and IPAFFS
  3. IPAFFS can find the ePhyto
  4. The ePhyto is used to populate parts of your import notification including your phytosanitary certificate and any data and documents uploaded by your exporter or phytosanitary health inspector to their export system.

You can choose to clone documents only (sometimes called 'verifiable pdfs') or data and documents.

When you clone :

  • data cloned will overwrite existing data you've entered
  • documents cloned will be in addition to documents already uploaded

You will not be able to delete cloned documents from your notification, but you can upload additional documents.
