Find out how cloning can save time

Cloning sets up a link between the exporter's system and IPAFFS. Use your electronic EHC or ePhyto to populate parts of your import notification. This includes your Health certificate and any data and documents uploaded by your exporter or vet to their export system (for example TracesNT). They must save it as an electronic certificate type for you to be able to use this feature.

This can save time compared to entering all the information manually and can cut down on manual errors and border checks triggered when data doesn't match up.
