How does cloning help?

Cloning can reduce manual errors and border checks that trigger when data does not match. It involves the following steps:

  1. Enter the digital health certificate reference number.
  2. IPAFFS links with the exporter's system, such as Traces NT.
  3. If found, the digital health certificate can complete parts of your import notification. You can include your health certificate and any data and documents uploaded by your exporter or vet to their export system.

You can choose to clone data and documents, or documents only (sometimes called ‘verifiable pdfs’).

When you clone:

  • data will overwrite information already entered
  • documents will be added to your notification
