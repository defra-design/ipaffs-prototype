You may save time by cloning from your electronic export health certificate (EHC) to populate your notification.

How does cloning save time?

Cloning can save you time by reducing manual errors and border checks that are triggered when data doesn't match up. It involves the following steps:

  1. You enter the electronic EHC or ePhyto reference number
  2. It sets up a link between the exporter's system (such as Traces NT) and IPAFFS
  3. IPAFFS can find the electronic certificate
  4. The electronic certificate is used to populate parts of your import notification including your health or phytosanitary certificate and any data and documents uploaded by your exporter or vet to their export system.

When you clone :

  • information cloned will overwrite existing information you've entered
  • documents cloned will be in addition to documents already uploaded

You can choose to clone documents only (sometimes called 'verifiable pdfs') or information and documents.
